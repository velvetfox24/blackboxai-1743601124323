4D Takki Rules

Objective

Be the first player to either:

  • Get 4 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on any single platform
  • Place one mark on each of the 4 platforms

Gameplay

  • Players alternate turns placing their mark (X or O)
  • Each turn, select any empty cell on any platform
  • You cannot place two marks on the same platform consecutively
